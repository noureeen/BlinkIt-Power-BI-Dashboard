# BlinkIt-Power-BI-Dashboard

## KPI Snapshot (Last 9 Months)

Here’s a quick snapshot of how the business is performing at a glance:

* **Total Revenue:** 27.34M
* **Quantity Sold (2024):** ~4K units
* **Year-on-Year Growth:** -7.46% (highlighting a clear improvement opportunity)
* **Marketing Reach:** 25.17M impressions
* **Conversions:** 252.96K successful actions
* **Inventory Health:** ~64% stock available
* **Damaged Stock:** ~3% (relatively controlled)
* **Customer Ratings:** Most feedback falls between **3 and 4**, showing average to good satisfaction

## Overview

This project is a **hands-on Power BI dashboard** created as part of my data analytics portfolio. It simulates a real-world **quick-commerce business scenario inspired by BlinkIt**, focusing on how data can be used to understand

## Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI Desktop** – Data modeling, visualization, and dashboard creation
* **DAX** – Measures and calculated insights
* **Data Cleaning & Transformation** – Power Query

---

## Dashboard Sections & Insights

### 1. Sales Overview

* Compares **current year vs previous year** sales and quantity (last 9 months)
* Tracks overall **growth (-7.46%)** to highlight performance decline
* Monthly trend analysis to identify peak and low-performing periods
* Identifies **top-selling products, customers, and locations**

Key Metrics:

* Quantity Sold (2023 vs 2024)
* Monthly Growth Trend
* Top Products & Areas by Quantity

---

### 2. Customer Overview

* Segments customers into **New, Regular, Premium, and Inactive**
* Tracks **repeat customers**, lost customers, and customer growth month-over-month
* Highlights **high-value customers** based on order frequency

Key Insights:

* Customers ordering more than 5 times
* Monthly customer growth comparison (2023 vs 2024)
* Top customers by quantity ordered

---

### 3. Customer Feedback Analysis

* Analyzes customer ratings from **1 to 5 stars**
* Categorizes feedback into **Positive, Neutral, and Negative**
* Breaks down issues by:

  * Delivery
  * Customer Service
  * Product Quality
  * App Experience

This helps identify **pain points impacting customer satisfaction** and areas that need operational improvement.

---

### 4. Inventory Management

* Tracks **stock received, available stock, and damaged stock**
* Calculates **available stock percentage (64%+)** and **damaged stock percentage (~3%)**
* Visualizes monthly stock movement and availability

Key Benefits:

* Prevents stock shortages
* Identifies inventory loss due to damage
* Improves stock planning and utilization

---

### 5. Marketing Performance

* Measures marketing efficiency using:

  * Clicks
  * Conversions
  * Impressions
  * Revenue Generated
  * Spend

Key Highlights:

* Over **17M impressions** and **170K+ conversions**
* Revenue and spend trends by month
* Helps evaluate campaign effectiveness and ROI

---

## Key Business Value

* Provides a **360° view of business performance**
* Enables data-driven decisions across sales, marketing, and operations
* Helps identify growth opportunities and operational bottlenecks
* Improves customer satisfaction through feedback analysis
